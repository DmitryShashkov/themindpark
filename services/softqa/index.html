Software Quality Assurance

Independent testing means quality

We house over 50 Quality Assurance Engineers, all of who work for our QA and software testing department. Our QA department remains an independent division within our group, and our engineers have completed more than 40 QA projects successfully. We have integrated and standalone testing solutions which we deliver to our clients from large corporations to software product startups. We have a deep understanding of your business applications and goals, allowing us to offer exceptional QA assurance.

Independent Quality Assurance offers a threefold benefit

Easy, fast evaluation
of product's compliance

Improved quality control
of an application

Insight into user impressions
of the product

Our QA Department applies RUP (Agile and Rational Unified Process) best practices in an effort to ensure we deliver quality work and to reduce the time to market for our client's software products. We do everything to ensure a satisfactory and timely launch of a product in them most efficient manner and to benefit our client.

Quality Assurance Services We Offer includes

Documentation

We deliver concise, properly structured documentation for a variety of niche audiences.

Full-Cycle QA

Our QA services are full cycle and make a natural progression from product analysis, development, implementation, sustaining/maintenance, and other solutions.

Pre-Certification and Compliance

We deliver and meet compliance testing, pre-certification and PCI, Section 508, DSS, and European as well as United Kingdom accessibility standards.

QA Consulting

We analyze your project and supply you with fact-based, experience-oriented solutions and recommendations for improvement.

Software Testing

As part of our testing services, we create a mix of every application. The mix includes server side systems, mobile apps, desktop, and Web. This allows us to ensure a greater degree of product consistency.

Test Automation

We develop customized testing solutions and we offer out of the box automation selection. In doing so, we can establish the best mix of infrastructure, technology, and goals.
