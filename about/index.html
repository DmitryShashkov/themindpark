About Us

We are a software development outsourcing and outstaffing company

We are a software development outsourcing and outstaffing company with a software development center in Kyiv, Ukraine and a representative US office in Wilmington, Delaware. Our core business is to establish and service fully managed dedicated software development teams in Ukraine, as well as deliver custom software development projects for our customers world-wide.

Why Us

Customer-Oriented Approach

You will find our services customer oriented as our goal is to satisfy our customers and to add to the value of your existing business. We meet your preferences by adapting our processes to fit your company's needs. We want to ensure your comfort when you outsource from us.

Our professional project managers will serve as expert extensions of your existing organization and this allows us to blend into your operations as we bring cost effective solutions to your operation by identifying the processes you can optimize to improve your bottom line.

We help you achieve savings and we seek to develop a long-term business relationship with every one of our clients.

Stable Qualified Teams

We nurture, attract and retain the top IT talent in the industry by having top grading HR, and through collaboration with the leading IT universities.

We offer an impressive benefits package, and we have grown a team consisting of well over 1300 IT experts at the ready to address your organization's needs. Each member of our team has a minimum of three and as many as 25 years IT experience working with software development projects.

Our turnover rate is minimal so you can count on your data, information, and intellectual property's security at all times.

Reasonable Cost
